## Key document relationships

- **Collision file:** CR-CS-005 + CR-CS-006 + CR-CS-007 must be filed together. CR-CS-007 is the zero-pointing document.
- **Driver qualification file:** CR-DQ-001 through CR-DQ-015 collectively. CR-DQ-002 is the checklist confirming all others are present.
- **Drug and alcohol:** CR-POL-010 (policy) must be signed by driver — signed copy filed in CR-DQ file.
- **DG drivers:** CR-DQ-008 (training verification) + CR-TDG-001 (training record) both required in driver file.
- **Brake inspectors:** CR-DQ-015 (qualification record) + CR-VM-008 (technical standard) — inspector must be certified per PMVI regulations.
